Include Participants of?

Instead of always creating a new participant list for each survey, you may also include the participants of previously existing surveys in your new survey.

This function can save you time in that you can interview existing participant lists with a variety of survey designs.

Don't want to include existing Participants?

If you don't want to include participants from other surveys you are able to not select any participants and just create a new list once the design has been established.
